AltaTV OTT Player

Watch movies and series online with real-time features and seamless video playback.

App Overview

AltaTV OTT Player allows users to stream movies and series online with a seamless and smooth video player experience. Just like other popular OTT platforms, it provides functionalities such as real-time watch time updates, easy switching between episodes in a series, and the ability to like or dislike content. Enjoy a smooth video player experience with the ability to seek forward and backward like Netflix and other leading streaming services.

Key Features

  • Stream Movies and Series: Watch high-quality movies and series directly from the app with an intuitive and smooth video player.
  • Like/Dislike Content: Rate content with a simple like/dislike feature.
  • Real-Time Watch Time: Keep track of your watch time with real-time updates.
  • Seamless Video Playback: Just like other OTT platforms, easily seek through videos, pause, and resume as needed.
  • Switch Between Episodes: Easily navigate between episodes in series with an intuitive interface.
  • Quality Changes: Quality changable in all content
  • Animated Episode Changes Automatic change episode to next after over present episode
